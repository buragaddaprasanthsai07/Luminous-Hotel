OUR LEGACY

The Dawn of an Era

Since its inception over a century ago, Luminous Hotel has stood as a symbol of architectural grandeur and refined living. Born from a vision to bring world-class luxury to the heart of the city, our halls have hosted royalty, dignitaries, and global visionaries.

We do not just offer rooms; we offer a gateway into a rich tapestry of history, where traditional cultural roots seamlessly blend with modern, serene comforts. Every archway and corridor tells a story of an era gone by.

A Century of Milestones

Trace the historic highlights that shaped Luminous from its initial foundation to the modern day.

  • 1912

    The Foundation

    Originally opened as a royal guest lodge, introducing the city to grand banquets and high-society gatherings.

  • 1935

    Hosting the Royals

    Hosting grand banquets for regional royalty and diplomatic envoys, defining early city elite hospitality.

  • 1956

    The Golden Era Expansion

    Added the historic east wing and the grand ballroom, establishing ourselves as the premier host for international summits.

  • 1980

    Culinary Legends Debut

    Opened our first legendary fine dining establishment, transforming the local gastronomic scene with generations of royal recipes.

  • 2002

    A Modern Revival

    Underwent a holistic restoration program, introducing smart home luxury suites while preserving all heritage hand-carved arches.

  • 2026

    Rebirth as Luminous

    Rebranded to Luminous, entering a new century of luxury, visual excellence, and world-class service standards.
